About Me

I am a medical doc turned McKinsey consultant turned startup founder and frontend hacker. I am the co-founder of Snappr, the YCombinator-backed on-demand marketplace for pro photography. Formerly CEO of GownTown, Australia's largest online graduation retailer.

I was previously a Consultant at McKinsey and Company, where my work was on strategy in consumer goods, healthcare, telecommunications/technology and education, with a particular focus on young and high-growth companies. My particular areas of expertise are portfolio strategy, pricing, customer segmentation strategy, sales force design, and marketing strategy.

I am a medicine and philosophy graduate of the University of New South Wales (UNSW), where I have been a tutor and lecturer, Assistant Dean of a residential college, and a researcher in neurosurgery. I am the founder of the Australian Medical Student Journal, a not-for-profit academic publication, and was the manager of EasyChem, a free online resource for HSC Chemistry students.
